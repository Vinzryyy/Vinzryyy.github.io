The Debut of KLP48

       
AKB48 Group Live in KL 2024 ~ First Cry ~ is an international concert was held 
on August 18, 2024 in Zepp Kuala Lumpur, Kuala Lumpur, Malaysia. It featured 
AKB48, STU48, JKT48, QUADLIPS, and KLP48.             

        This event, AKB48 Group Live in KL 2024 ~ First Cry ~, marks a significant milestone in the AKB48 Group's international outreach. 
        Held at Zepp Kuala Lumpur on August 18, 2024, the concert showcases the collaboration and diversity of the AKB48 Group's various branches, 
        including the debut of Malaysia's KLP48. The lineup featured:
            AKB48 (Japan)
            STU48 (Japan)
            JKT48 (Indonesia)
            QUADLIPS (Thailand)
            KLP48 (Malaysia)
        
        Event Highlights:
            1.Each group delivered standout performances of their most popular tracks, along with a few unreleased songs.
            2.KLP48’s debut stood out with performances in Malay, Japanese, and Chinese, emphasizing their cultural versatility.
            3.The collaborative finale featured all groups performing "Aitakatta (会いたかった)", symbolizing unity across cultures.

Memorable Moments in Photos

This concert follows AKB48 Group Circle Jam 2023, continuing the tradition of showcasing the group’s growing international presence as part of the Asia Festival series. It was also the first official stage for KLP48, cementing Malaysia’s inclusion in the AKB48 Group network.
